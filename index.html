Grittibänz!

1. Collect your ingredients

Get all your utils together and wash your hands!

1.1. Don't forget the yeast!

Yeast is a very important ingredient so make sure you have it.

2. Measure the flour

Mix the flour with salt and sugar in a bowl.

2.1. Mix, mix, mix!

Mix for about 5 minutes.

3. Add yeast

Add the yeast to the mixture and keep mixing.

(If you use cubes make sure to crumble it whilst mixing.)

4. Measure the milk

Measure the milk in order to mix it under the mixture

4.1. Fill in the milk

Fill in the milk and add butter before you mix.

4.2. The big mix

As you have this mixture,

mix it for about 10 minutes until you have a soft and clean mass.

4.3. Use your clean hands

Knead everything together to get that soft mass.

5. Let it rest!

Put the mass in a room temperature and dry corner in the kitchen.

5.1. Pro Tip!

Cover the bowl with a wet towel so that it can open properly.

Wait about 2 hrs.

6. Form shapes

After 2 hrs, form now fun shapes with the dough.

You can cut slots with a knife to form the arms, legs and the head.

7. Stir the egg

Stir the egg to spread it afterwards.

7.1. Spread the egg

Spread the egg evenly onto the bread.

8. Preheat the oven

Preheat the oven at 180 degrees for about 15 minutes.

After 15 minutes bake the "Grittibänz" for another 15 miunutes.

9. "Grittibänz" out of the oven

Take the Grittibänz out of the oven and let it cool down for about an 1 hr.
