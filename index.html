Ingredient Amount
Salt 1tsp
Yeast 1/2 cube
Butter 50g
Milk 2.5dl
Egg 1
Raisins 50g
Almond Sticks 50g
Chocolate 50g
Sugar Flakes 50g

Instructions

mix flour, salt, sugar and yeast in a bowl

Add the butter and milk, mix, knead for approx. 10 mins. to form a soft, smooth dough. Cover and leave to rise at room temperature for approx. 2 hrs. until doubled in size.

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.

Divide the dough into 4 portions, shape each into a roll, cut off approx. 1/5 for hair and decorations. Cut out the head, arms and legs. Glue the decorations on with a little water. Place the pancakes on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Beat the egg, brush the pancakes with it, decorate with raisins and almond sticks, pressing them well into the dough.

Bake for approx. 25 mins. in the lower half of the oven. Remove and leave to cool on a wire rack.

Decorate with chunks of chocolate and sugar flakes by choice

Enjoy!
